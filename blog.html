Could a Robot Dentist Be the Future of Dental Care?

30 JUL 2024

Perceptive, an innovative startup, is transforming the dental industry with a groundbreaking robot designed to make dental procedures faster, safer, and more precise. This cutting-edge robot uses advanced imaging technology to map teeth with incredible accuracy, streamlining processes like crown placement and making them more efficient. Prioritizing safety, the robot moves in sync with patients to prevent any errors, all while being overseen by a dentist. Although it might be a few years before these robots become a staple in dental offices, they have the potential to significantly reduce the anxiety many feel about dental visits.

Self-Driving Cars Cause Gridlock in San Francisco

August 2024

San Francisco recently witnessed a traffic jam caused by a fleet of self-driving taxis from Cruise. These autonomous vehicles suddenly stopped in the middle of the street, seemingly for no reason, leaving passengers stranded and human drivers frustrated. The vehicles “froze” simultaneously, creating confusion and highlighting the ongoing challenges of autonomous driving technology in busy urban settings. This incident underscores the difficulties these systems face when navigating complex environments, where unexpected issues can quickly spiral into traffic chaos
